> **"Governing is easy; the hard part is finishing your term without a helicopter waiting on the roof."**

**Municipal Madness** is a political strategy and management simulator built with **Next.js 15**. You step into the shoes of a Mayor in a fictional district, balancing public finances, social approval, and the dark threads of corruption in a dynamic environment filled with satirical dilemmas.

---

## üïπÔ∏è Gameplay Experience

The project combines city-builder mechanics with narrative decision-making:

* **Leader Selection**: 4 unique profiles (The Entrepreneur, The Militant, The Union Leader, and The Influencer) with specific attributes that shape your early game.
* **Triple-Impact Management**:
    * üíµ **Budget**: The municipal treasury. No money, no public works (or campaign barbecues).
    * ‚ù§Ô∏è **Popularity**: Your political life insurance.
    * üíº **Corruption**: A dangerous shortcut to quick cash, but a straight line to federal intervention.
* **Dilemma Engine**: Random events (strikes, potholes, "family favors") requiring immediate decisions with systemic consequences.
* **Interactive Map**: Real-time visualization of inaugurated public works.
* **Vox Populi**: A live citizen feedback feed that reacts to your policies and charisma levels.
